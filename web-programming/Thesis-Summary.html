Jeremy Bass

Autonomous Vehicle

Futures.

To drive adoption of autonomous vehicles, the design of user experiences should be treated as ‘interactions with robots’, not as cars. Trustworthy autonomous vehicle-based services are possible if the user experiences are modeled after design principles from the field of human-robot interaction.

In the research phase of this project, my primary concern was to uncover user hesitations and expections for riding in an autonomous vehicle. This included simulations, improv sessions and an interactive full-scale model.

The prototyping phase involved creating more interactive research experiences. This included an autonomous human suit (android), and the building of an autonomous vehicle. This functional vehicle was used to validate user concerns as well as uncover new insights.
